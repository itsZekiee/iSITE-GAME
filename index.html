Matching Cards

"ByteMatch" is a card matching game that tests your knowledge of programming languages. The game has three difficulty levels: easy, average, and hard. Each difficulty level has a time limit and a limited number of moves. Players must find the matching pairs of programming language icons within the allotted time and moves. The top 5 players on the leaderboard will receive rewards from the iSITE Community!
